AI-Powered Brain MRI Analysis with MindScan MindScan assists medical professionals and researchers in detecting brain anomalies such as tumors or lesions from MRI scans. It uses deep learning and advanced image preprocessing to highlight suspicious regions and improve diagnostic efficiency.
Intelligent Preprocessing Enhancement of MRI scans for clearer visualization.
Smart Segmentation AI-based segmentation to highlight potential tumor or lesion areas.
Accurate Insights Get confidence scores and visual overlays for each MRI slice analyzed.
